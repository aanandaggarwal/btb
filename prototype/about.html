Our History

Launched in 2020, Beyond the Beats was born out of a small online forum where hip-hop enthusiasts shared rare finds from the genre's underground scene. As interest grew, so did our ambition to create a dedicated platform for these voices. Today, we're proud to be a go-to resource for fans and artists alike, charting the evolution of hip-hop through thoughtful curation and commentary. Our journey is a testament to the vibrant community that supports us, and we're excited for what the future holds.
