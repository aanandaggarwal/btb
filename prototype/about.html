About Us

Aanand Aggarwal

Founder & Editor

With a passion for underground and alternative hip-hop, Aanand launched Beyond the Beats to explore the depths of the genre and share its rich culture. Aanand oversees editorial direction and contributes in-depth reviews, ensuring the content not only resonates with hardcore fans but also welcomes newcomers to the scene.

Mia Zhang

Content Director

Mia curates the featured artist galleries and manages our contributor network. Her keen eye for emerging talent helps keep Beyond the Beats at the forefront of hip-hop journalism. Mia's commitment to authenticity and her proactive approach to content curation are instrumental in shaping our editorial vision.

Elijah Kim

Technical Lead

Elijah's technical expertise keeps our site running smoothly. He's the mastermind behind the interactive elements that make our galleries and timelines engaging for visitors. His innovative solutions and forward-thinking approach ensure that Beyond the Beats remains at the cutting edge of digital publishing.
