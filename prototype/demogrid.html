Legendary Hip-Hop Artists

MF DOOM was known for his intricate lyrics and unique mask, which he wore as part of his iconic image. He influenced a generation with his complex narratives and abstract rhymes.

Quasimoto, also known as Lord Quas, is an alter ego of Madlib. This character is known for his high-pitched voice and jazzy, lo-fi beats which add a distinct texture to his storytelling.

The Pharcyde is a group celebrated for their light-hearted lyrics and innovative approach to hip-hop. Their work includes critical acclaim for albums like 'Bizarre Ride II the Pharcyde'.
